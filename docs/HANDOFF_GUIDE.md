## The Dashboard / لوحة التحكم

When you log in, you'll see your **Dashboard**. It has:

1. **Numbers** at the top — how many menu items, reviews, and photos you have
2. **Quick Action Cards** — click any card to edit that section

The sidebar on the left shows all sections you can edit:
- **Homepage Welcome** — the big message visitors see first
- **About Me** — your story and specialties
- **My Work Experience** — your career history (roles, workplaces)
- **Menu Items** — your bakes and prices
- **Services** — consulting, training, and baking services
- **Customer Reviews** — what people say about you
- **Photo Gallery** — pictures of your work
- **Service Areas** — where you're based
- **Contact Info** — phone, email, address, WhatsApp

## How to Update Each Section / كيفية تحديث كل قسم

### Homepage Welcome / الرسالة الترحيبية

This is the first thing visitors see. It has:
- **Main Title** — big headline (English + Arabic)
- **Short Description** — text below the title (English + Arabic)
- **Button Text** — what the main button says (e.g. "View Our Menu")

**To edit:**
1. Click "Homepage Welcome" in the sidebar
2. Change any text field
3. Click "Save Changes" at the bottom

---

### About Me / عني

This is your story. It has:
- **Title** — e.g. "Hi, I'm Chef Mohamed" (English + Arabic)
- **Your Story** — the full text about your journey (English + Arabic)
- **Your Photo** — upload a picture of yourself
- **What You Do Best** — your specialties (add/remove skills)
- **Stats** — achievement badges like "10+ Years Professional"
- **Tagline** — a short phrase under your name

**To add a skill or stat:**
1. Type it in the box
2. Press Enter or click ➕
3. Click X to remove one

---

### Menu Items / قائمة الطعام

This is your bakery menu. Each item has:
- **Name** (English + Arabic)
- **Description** (English + Arabic)
- **Price** — leave empty for "Price on request"
- **Category** — Breads, Cakes, Pastries, Cookies, or Seasonal
- **Available** — toggle ON to show on website, OFF to hide

**To add an item:**
1. Click "Add Item"
2. Fill in the form
3. Click "Add Item"

**To reorder:** Drag the ⋮⋮ handle up or down

**To delete:** Click the 🗑️ icon — confirm in the popup

---

### Services / الخدمات

This is where you list your consulting, training, and baking services. Each service has:
- **Service Type** — Artisanal Baking, Consulting, or Training
- **Service Name** (English + Arabic)
- **Description** — what's included (English + Arabic)
- **Emoji Icon** — pick a small icon like 🥐 or 📋
- **Show on Website** — toggle ON to display

**To add a service:**
1. Click "Add Service"
2. Fill in the form
3. Click "Add Service"

**To reorder:** Drag the ⋮⋮ handle up or down

---

### Customer Reviews / تقييمات العملاء

These are quotes from people who love your work. Each review has:
- **Customer Name** — who said it
- **Rating** — click 1 to 5 stars
- **Quote** (English + Arabic)
- **Show on Website** — toggle ON to display

Reviews are hidden by default until you toggle them ON.

---

### Photo Gallery / معرض الصور

This is where you show pictures of your bakes.

**To add photos:**
1. Click "Add Photos"
2. Type captions (optional) in English and Arabic
3. Click "Choose images" — select one or more photos from your computer
4. Wait for upload to finish

**Requirements:**
- File types: JPG or PNG work best
- Max size: 5 MB per photo
- If a photo is too big, resize it on your phone first

**To reorder:** Drag the ⋮⋮ handle on each photo

**To delete:** Hover over a photo, click the 🗑️ icon

---

### Contact Info / معلومات التواصل

This is how customers reach you. Fields:
- **Phone** — your primary number
- **Phone (Secondary)** — backup number / WhatsApp
- **WhatsApp Link** — starts with `https://wa.me/...`
- **Response Time** — e.g. "24 hours guaranteed" (English + Arabic)
- **Email** — your email address
- **Instagram** — your handle (without @)
- **Address** (English + Arabic)
- **Booking / Catering URL** — link for custom orders

## Arabic Translations / الترجمة العربية

All Arabic translations on your website have been completed by a professional translator.
**You don't need to translate anything.** Every text field already has both English and Arabic versions.

If you edit a field in English, make sure to also fill in the Arabic version next to it (look for the box with `dir="rtl"` — text goes right-to-left). If you're unsure, ask your developer.

### What was translated:
- All homepage text (welcome, descriptions, buttons)
- Your full biography
- Menu items and prices
- Services descriptions
- Admin panel labels and navigation
- Gallery captions, work experience entries, and contact information

## Theme / المظهر

Your website has two color themes:

- **Dark Mode** (default) — dark background, gold accents
- **Light Mode** — cream background, warm brown text

To switch: Click the ☀️/🌙 icon in the top bar (next to the language switcher).

On mobile: Open the menu ☰ first, then tap the theme icon.

---

## Language / اللغة

To switch between English and Arabic:
- Click `EN` or `عربي` in the top bar
- On mobile: Open the menu ☰ first

The page will reload in the other language. All your content stays the same — only the labels change.

## Glossary / قاموس المصطلحات

*Developer words → Chef-friendly meaning*

| Technical Term | What It Means |
|----------------|---------------|
| **Admin Panel** | The private dashboard where you edit your website |
| **Bilingual** | Available in both English and Arabic |
| **Dashboard** | The first screen you see after logging in |
| **Deploy** | Publish changes to the live website |
| **Environment Variables** | Secret codes the website needs to work (set by developer) |
| **Mutation** | A save action — "mutation" just means "save to database" |
| **RTL** | Right-to-left — how Arabic text flows on the page |
| **Schema** | The structure of your data (set by developer) |
| **Seed** | Add starter content to a new website |
| **Session** | Your login status — "session expired" means "log in again" |

## Need Help? / هل تحتاج مساعدة؟

If something isn't working:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
3. Try logging out and logging back in

If it's still broken, contact your developer with:
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- A screenshot if possible

**Developer contact:** [Your developer's name / email / phone]
